Blot to Frigga

HAIL FRIGGA

    I praise thee, Allmother, for thy love and tenderness to me.

    Hail, Frithweaver, bringer of peace to my heart and hearth.

    Hail, Queen of Heaven, for reminding me of my potential as a woman.

    Thanks to thee for guiding me to this Heathen path.

    Thanks to thee for guiding us to this house.

    Thanks to thee for this past month of study, for coming to know you better.

    Please join me for this wine.  may our relationship grow through the years.
