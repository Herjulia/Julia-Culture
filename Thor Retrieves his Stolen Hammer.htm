The Story

Thor was angry when he awoke to find his hammer, Mjolnir, missing.  He began to shake, then fumbled around.

"Loki!", he shouted.  "Loki, my hammer is stolen!"

Loki agreed to go with him to Freya's bower.  Thor hoped Freya would loan him her feather coat, so he could fly and look for his hammer.

They met Freya.  "Will you lend me your feather coat, so I can find my hammer quickly?"

Freya was generous with her coat.  "Even if it were made of gold or silver, because you ask, Thor, I will give it to you."

Loki donned the feather coat, and flew far beyond the halls of the gods, to the lands of the giants.  He found Thrym, the Lord of the thurses, sitting on a mound.  He was making golden leashes for his dogs, and was sleeking his horses manes.
