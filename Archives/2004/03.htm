Witchcraft:  This is hard to nail down.  I think there are as many forms of witchcraft as there are people who practice it, but I will start to describe what I see as the main features.

Spiritual paths and the mindset that follows

The one thing I have found all spiritual paths seem to have in common, is that whether the adherents acknowledge it or not, they think they have the edge on spiritual truth.  Even those who proclaim vehemently that they have nothing against other religions or spiritual traditions, or that there path is not religious, just spiritual, and that they are open to the spiritual experience of any person.  If you start talking to them, before long, it will become apparent that they view you as needing to be brought up to speed on "real" spiritual wisdom, and that it can really be found by doing what they do.

I guess by wandering around and asking a lot of questions, I always feel like my point of view is open to debate.  I hope that feeling keeps me from this kind of spiritual arrogance.  I hope it keeps me able to hear wise words when they are offered, no matter who they come from.
