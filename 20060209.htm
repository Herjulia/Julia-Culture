1)  Warding the space:

I perform the hammer sign and call on Asa Thor to provide spiritual protection during this rite.  I also call on Peacock for His protection.* In the air, I trace the following runes:  Tiwaz to the north; Othalaz to the west; Ansuz to the south; and Kenaz to the east.  I start a small fire, sprinkled with sage and rosemary.

2)  Standing in Elhaz, I address the Bane of the Pharisees:

 

"Before-times, I followed you, Pierced One.

At times, I was helped by you and your saints.

At times, my relatives, Grandpa Stahl, Aunt Helen, and Sr. Christopher, were examples to me of kindness, gentleness, intelligence, and every thing wise, high-minded that your followers might be.

I am grateful for what you have done in guiding me on this Earth.

"A Protector, you have not been, and those who lead in your name are mostly faithless to you.

This religion binds me, shrinks me, weakens me, and offends me.

"Shepherd, I follow you no longer.

I will not follow your Sire, the Desert God.

Let the Fire of your Spirit return to you.

"I call upon my ancestors as witnesses:

I DO NOT FOLLOW THE TRIUNE GOD.

(At this point, I will trace Naudiz on the Bible, then set it on the fire)

"From this day forward, I am my own.

I embrace the blood in my own veins, the sacred spirit that engendered it, and my ancestors who have given it to me.

This path, these spiritual gifts, this life and breath, are all mine, my gifts, to do with as I choose.

My thoughts are my own.

My deeds are my own.

My life is my own.

Go in Peace, Son of David.

3)  Conclusion:

(Pouring the appropriate allotments of beer)

Hail the Aesir!

Hail the Vanir!

Hail my ancestors, who have lead me here, and witnessed this deed.  May I learn to honor you!

Hail to the Heathens, past and present, especially those who help me now, teaching and guiding me in this newly refound faith.

*Though not particularly Heathen, Peacock is a spiritual helper to me, known as a Power Animal.
