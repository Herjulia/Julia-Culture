Spiritual "dating"

'What?  Like "carbon dating"?  You want to bring spirituality into your dating?  I thought you were in a committed relationship?'

Now simmer down.  I had this "aha" moment today, when I realized there is a dimension of my spiritual exploration that is like dating.  In my case, like guys.  So I will be adding the "adolescent crush" analysis to the cerebral and analytical information on each spiritual topic.

DISCLAIMER:  this part is in no way intended to be prescriptive or to say this is how I feel through and through.  I'm attempting to extrovert some of my sillier and less rational "stuff" so I can get at the core of what I really am looking for spiritually.
